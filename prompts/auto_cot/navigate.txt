Q: If you follow these instructions, do you return to the starting point? Turn around. Take 4 steps. Turn around. Take 4 steps.
Options:
- Yes
- No
A: Let's think step by step.
First, you turn around. This means you are facing the opposite direction from where you started. Then, you take 4 steps forward. After that, you turn around again. Now, you are facing the same direction as when you started. Finally, you take 4 steps forward again.

So, if you follow these instructions, you will return to the starting point. So the answer is yes.

Q: If you follow these instructions, do you return to the starting point? Always face forward. Take 6 steps left. Take 3 steps backward. Take 3 steps left.

- Yes
- No
A: Let's think step by step.
First, we start facing forward. Then, we take 6 steps to the left. This means we have moved to the left of the starting point. Next, we take 3 steps backward. This means we have moved back towards the starting point. Finally, we take 3 steps to the left again. This means we have moved even further to the left.

Based on these instructions, we do not return to the starting point. So the answer is no.
