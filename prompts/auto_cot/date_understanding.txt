Q: Today's meeting is rescheduled to 11 am tomorrow, 10/16/1924. What is the date one week ago from today in MM/DD/YYYY?
Options:
(A) 08/08/1925
(B) 10/08/2019
(C) 11/06/1924
(D) 04/08/1925
(E) 10/08/1924
(F) 10/07/1924
A: Let's think step by step.
First, we need to determine today's date. The given date is 10/16/1924.

Next, we need to find the date one week ago from today. To do this, we subtract 7 days from the given date.

10/16/1924 - 7 days = 10/09/1924

Therefore, the date one week ago from today is 10/09/1924.

So the answer is (E).
