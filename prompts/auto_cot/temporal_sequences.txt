Q: Today, William went to the soccer field. Between what times could they have gone?
We know that:
William woke up at 1pm.
Sarah saw William working at the office from 1pm to 2pm.
Mary saw William waiting at the train station from 2pm to 4pm.
Susan saw William taking photos near the Eiffel Tower from 8pm to 10pm.
The soccer field was closed after 10pm.
Between what times could William have gone to the soccer field?
Options:
(A) 8pm to 10pm
(B) 1pm to 2pm
(C) 2pm to 4pm
(D) 4pm to 8pm
A: Let's think step by step.
First, we know that William woke up at 1pm. So, he could have gone to the soccer field anytime after 1pm.

Next, Sarah saw William working at the office from 1pm to 2pm. This means that William could not have gone to the soccer field during this time.

Then, Mary saw William waiting at the train station from 2pm to 4pm. This means that William could not have gone to the soccer field during this time either.

Finally, Susan saw William taking photos near the Eiffel Tower from 8pm to 10pm. This means that William could have gone to the soccer field anytime before 8pm.

So the answer is (D).
