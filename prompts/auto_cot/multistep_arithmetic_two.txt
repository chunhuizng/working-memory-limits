Next, let's simplify the expression inside the parentheses on the right side:

-3 - 5 * 3 + -7 = -3 - 15 + -7 = -18 + -7 = -25

((-5) + (-25)) = -5 - 25 = -30

Therefore, ((-2 - -7 - 5 * 2) + (-3 - 5 * 3 + -7)) = -30.

So the answer is -30.
