Q: Alice, Bob, Claire, Dave, and Eve are on the same team in a soccer match. At the start of the match, they are each assigned to a position: Alice is playing goalkeeper, Bob is playing striker, Claire is playing cheerleader, Dave is playing center midfielder, and Eve is playing right midfielder.
As the game progresses, pairs of players occasionally swap positions. First, Eve and Dave trade positions. Then, Dave and Bob trade positions. Then, Dave and Claire trade positions. Then, Dave and Bob trade positions. Finally, Alice and Bob trade positions. At the end of the match, Claire is playing
Options:
(A) goalkeeper
(B) striker
(C) cheerleader
(D) center midfielder
(E) right midfielder
A: Let's think step by step.
At the start of the match, Claire is assigned as the cheerleader.

Then, the following swaps occur:
1. Eve and Dave trade positions. Now, Eve is the center midfielder and Dave is the right midfielder.
2. Dave and Bob trade positions. Now, Dave is the striker and Bob is the right midfielder.
3. Dave and Claire trade positions. Now, Dave is the cheerleader and Claire is the striker.
4. Dave and Bob trade positions. Now, Dave is the right midfielder and Bob is the cheerleader.
5. Alice and Bob trade positions. Now, Alice is the cheerleader and Bob is the goalkeeper.

Therefore, at the end of the match, Claire is playing as the striker.

So the answer is (B).

Q: Alice, Bob, Claire, Dave, and Eve are holding a white elephant gift exchange. At the start of the event, they are each holding a present of a different color: Alice has a red present, Bob has a pink ball, Claire has a black ball, Dave has a brown present, and Eve has a purple present.
As the event progresses, pairs of people swap gifts. First, Claire and Dave swap their gifts. Then, Eve and Dave swap their gifts. Then, Eve and Claire swap their gifts. Then, Dave and Bob swap their gifts. Finally, Alice and Claire swap their gifts. At the end of the event, Alice has the
Options:
(A) red present
(B) pink ball
(C) black ball
(D) brown present
(E) purple present
A: Let's think step by step.
First, Claire and Dave swap their gifts. This means Claire now has the brown present and Dave now has the black ball.

Next, Eve and Dave swap their gifts. This means Eve now has the black ball and Dave now has the purple present.

Then, Eve and Claire swap their gifts. This means Eve now has the brown present and Claire now has the purple present.

Next, Dave and Bob swap their gifts. This means Dave now has the pink ball and Bob now has the purple present.

Finally, Alice and Claire swap their gifts. This means Alice now has the purple present and Claire now has the red present.

Therefore, at the end of the event, Alice has the purple present.

So the answer is (E).
