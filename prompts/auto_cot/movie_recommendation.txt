Q: Find a movie similar to The Fugitive, Terminator 2 Judgment Day, Aladdin, Toy Story:
Options:
(A) The Edge of Love
(B) Untitled Spider-Man Reboot
(C) The Lion King
(D) Daddy Day Camp
A: Let's think step by step.
The Fugitive is a thriller/action movie about a man wrongly accused of a crime and trying to clear his name. Terminator 2 Judgment Day is a sci-fi/action movie about a cyborg sent back in time to protect a young boy from a deadly robot. Aladdin is an animated fantasy movie about a young street thief who finds a magical lamp and uses it to win the heart of a princess. Toy Story is an animated adventure movie about a group of toys that come to life when humans are not around.

Based on these descriptions, the movie that is most similar to The Fugitive, Terminator 2 Judgment Day, Aladdin, and Toy Story would be (C) The Lion King.
So the answer is (C).
